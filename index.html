Finding Eden

Where Wellness Finds You

Almost every person I meet eventually tells me something they’re carrying alone.

Anxiety. Trauma. Loss. Burnout. Confusion about where life is headed.

Most people don’t have a place to take those conversations.

Finding Eden exists to create that place.

A space where people can slow down, reconnect with themselves, and explore healing through creativity, movement, storytelling, and community.

What Finding Eden Is

Finding Eden is envisioned as a calming community environment where people can reconnect with themselves through creative expression, somatic movement, reflection, and shared human experience.

  • Somatic movement and dance
  • Creative art workshops
  • Story sharing circles
  • Sound and music experiences
  • Quiet calming spaces for reflection
  • Community healing events

Why This Matters

We are living in a time where people are more connected online than ever before, yet more emotionally isolated.

Many people carry trauma, grief, anxiety, and stress without safe places to process those experiences.

Clinical therapy helps many people, but it isn’t the only path to healing.

Movement heals. Creativity heals. Community heals.
