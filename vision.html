The Vision

Finding Eden is imagined as a welcoming community space designed to support emotional wellbeing through creativity, movement, storytelling, and human connection.

The long‑term goal is to create accessible experiences where people can slow down, reconnect with themselves, and explore different forms of healing.

This includes workshops, art spaces, somatic movement classes, storytelling circles, and community gatherings.
