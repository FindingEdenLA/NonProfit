Volunteer

Join the founding community.

Finding Eden is actively gathering volunteers, facilitators, artists, and community-minded people who want to help build something meaningful from the ground up.

Volunteer roles

  • Event support, setup, breakdown, and guest welcome.
  • Trauma-informed facilitators and workshop guides.
  • Artists, musicians, movement teachers, and storytellers.
  • Marketing, outreach, photography, and documentation.
  • Community ambassadors and donation/support helpers.

Volunteers will be contacted as launch planning develops and funding allows for additional paid support.
