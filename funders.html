Funders & partners

Help build Finding Eden from the ground up.

Finding Eden is a grassroots project building toward a permanent community healing center in Los Angeles. Support helps move the vision from concept to lived reality.

What support makes possible
  • Launch events and monthly pop-up workshops.
  • Stipends for trauma-informed facilitators and artists.
  • Supplies for art, journaling, movement, and sensory regulation.
  • Venue rentals, insurance, setup, accessibility needs, and documentation.
  • Future investment in a physical home base and core staff.

Ways to partner

Funding & grants

Support startup operations, launch events, pilot programs, and future site development.

In-kind support

Donate art supplies, floor cushions, tables, tea service, printing, venue support, media coverage, or volunteer hours.

Program sponsorship

Underwrite a circle, art lab, somatic class, or youth-centered offering in your name or organization’s name.

Ready to collaborate?

For funding conversations, partnership opportunities, or event support, contact Finding Eden directly and include a short note about your interest.
