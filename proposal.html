Proposal overview

A concise, funder-friendly overview of the Finding Eden vision.

This page can be shared with potential partners, donors, or grant contacts as a quick snapshot of what Finding Eden is building and why it matters.

Project summary

Finding Eden is a Los Angeles grassroots initiative building community spaces for healing, creativity, and connection through trauma-informed movement, art, storytelling, and collective support.

The near-term focus is launching community pop-up experiences and strengthening a founding volunteer and partner network. The long-term goal is a permanent home base where accessible healing programs can grow.

Need & relevance

Too many people are trying to process trauma, depression, grief, and chronic stress in isolation. Finding Eden responds to this need by creating spaces that feel more human, relational, and creatively engaging.

Phase 1

Launch event, pop-up workshops, volunteer activation, and initial documentation.

Phase 2

Recurring programs, stronger community partnerships, and facilitator support.

Phase 3

Funding pathway toward a permanent center and sustainable staffing.

Support opportunities

Funding, sponsorship, in-kind support, venue partnerships, media support, and aligned community collaboration are all welcome.
